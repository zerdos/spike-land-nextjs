### Description

**Main description for the listing page (260 characters optimal)**

spike.land is an AI-powered social media command center for solo creators,
small businesses, and freelance marketers.

Connect all your accounts - Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube,
Discord - and let AI help you create content, schedule posts, and track what
works.

No more juggling 6 different platforms. No more writer's block staring at
blank post boxes. No more guessing which content will perform.

Our AI learns your brand voice, suggests content ideas, optimizes post timing,
and runs A/B tests automatically. Built by a developer who needed to market his
own products and couldn't find tools that actually worked.

**Key Features**:

- AI content generation with brand voice consistency
- Multi-platform scheduling (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube,
  Discord)
- Automated A/B testing for posts
- Real-time analytics and engagement tracking
- MCP server integration for Claude Code/Desktop
- Live React environments for visual content creation

**Perfect for**:

- Solo content creators building an audience
- Small business owners managing their own marketing
- Freelance social media managers juggling multiple clients

Built with: Next.js 15, TypeScript, Prisma, Cloudflare Workers, Claude AI

## Hunter Strategy

**Why use a hunter?**

- Hunters with large followings can amplify your launch
- Some hunters specialize in specific categories (SaaS, AI, productivity)
- Hunter's network can mean better initial visibility

**How to find a hunter:**

1. Browse Product Hunt and note hunters who launch products in your category
2. Check their follower count (aim for 10k+)
3. Review their past launches (success rate, engagement quality)
4. Reach out 2-3 weeks before launch with a personalized message

**Message template:**

```
Hi [Hunter Name],

I'm Zoltan, founder of spike.land - an AI-powered social media command center
for solo creators and small businesses.

I'm planning to launch on Product Hunt in [timeframe], and I noticed you've
hunted several successful [category] products. I love your work on [specific
product they hunted].

Would you be interested in hunting spike.land? I think it would resonate with
your audience because [specific reason].

Happy to provide early access and answer any questions.

Thanks for considering!

Zoltan
spike.land
```

**Alternative: Self-launch**

Self-launching can be more authentic. You own the narrative. Direct relationship
with the community. Many successful launches are self-launched.

**Recommendation**: Try self-launch first. It's more work but builds genuine
community connection.

## Community Engagement Plan

### How to Respond to Comments

**Positive comments:**

- Thank them genuinely
- Ask follow-up question about their use case
- Offer to help them get started
- Example: "Thanks so much! Are you currently managing multiple social accounts?
  I'd love to help you get set up."

**Critical feedback:**

- Thank them for the feedback
- Acknowledge the concern
- Explain reasoning or share roadmap
- Example: "Great point about [feature]. We're planning to add that in Q2.
  Would you be interested in beta testing it?"

**Questions:**

- Answer thoroughly
- Link to relevant docs/resources
- Offer to continue conversation via DM/email
- Example: "Here's how it works: [detailed answer]. I put together a demo video
  here: [link]. Happy to jump on a call if helpful!"

**Feature requests:**

- Thank them for the suggestion
- Ask clarifying questions
- Add to public roadmap if makes sense
- Example: "Love this idea! Are you thinking [interpretation A] or
  [interpretation B]? I'll add it to our roadmap either way."

**Competitors mentioned:**

- Be professional and respectful
- Highlight what makes you different (not why they're bad)
- Example: "We love [competitor] too! Where spike.land differs is [specific
  advantage]. Both are great tools for different needs."

## FAQ Responses (Pre-prepared)

**Q: How is this different from Buffer/Hootsuite?**

A: spike.land is built with AI-first from the ground up. We don't just schedule
- we help you create content with your brand voice, run A/B tests automatically,
and integrate with developer tools like Claude Code. Plus, transparent
token-based pricing instead of per-seat subscriptions.

**Q: What platforms do you support?**

A: Currently: Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, Discord. We're
adding TikTok and Threads next quarter based on user demand.

**Q: Is my data secure?**

A: Yes. OAuth tokens are AES-256-GCM encrypted. We never store your passwords.
API calls use secure connections. Full privacy policy at spike.land/privacy.

**Q: How does pricing work?**

A: Token-based system. 1 token = ¬£0.01. AI-generated posts cost 2-5 tokens.
Image generation costs 2-10 tokens depending on quality. No monthly fees unless
you want a subscription for discounts. Pay for what you use.

**Q: Can I use this for clients?**

A: Absolutely! Freelance social media managers are a key persona. You can manage
multiple clients, each with their own brand voice and content calendar.

**Q: What's the MCP integration?**

A: MCP (Model Context Protocol) lets you use spike.land directly from Claude
Code or Claude Desktop. Generate content, schedule posts, check analytics - all
without leaving your AI workflow.

**Q: Do you offer a free trial?**

A: Yes! New users get 1,000 free tokens (¬£10 value) to test all features.
Enough for ~50 AI-generated posts. No credit card required.

**Q: Roadmap?**

A: Q1 2026: TikTok & Threads integration, advanced analytics, team features Q2
2026: WhatsApp Business, influencer outreach tools, API v2 Q3 2026: Affiliate
program, reseller options, enterprise features
