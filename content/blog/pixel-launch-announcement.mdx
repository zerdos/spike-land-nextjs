AI has changed everything.

Old VHS recordings. Faded paper photos. Grainy phone snapshots. Until recently, these were just... gone. The detail wasn't there to recover.

Now it is. Pixel uses Google's latest Gemini model to restore images to **hyper-realistic 4K**. Not upscaled. Restored.

You can see yourself young again. Clearer than you remember.
